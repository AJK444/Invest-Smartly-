Liquidate with Our Shares Transfer Facility

How It Works

In this option, you can sell your shares to other investors on the platform who are looking to purchase shares. This provides an opportunity to liquidate your shares and get value for them without waiting for their maturity.

Disclaimer

Please note that while Invest Smartly provides the Share Transfer Facility, it does not guarantee the sale of your shares. The platform enables you to list your shares, making them available for other investors to purchase.

You are eligible to purchase shares on/from/off the Share Transfer Facility if you have at least one active investment on the Invest Smartly Platform.

*Only shares that have been on the platform for more than six months are qualified to be listed on the Shares Transfer Facility.
